ABOUT BHG

BHG Energy and Minerals Limited is a leading exploration company dedicated to the responsible and sustainable extraction of mineral resources in Tanzania.

Our Mission

To unlock the potential of Tanzania's mineral resources through advanced exploration techniques and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To be Tanzania's premier energy and minerals company, delivering innovative solutions in exploration and extraction.

Our Values

Sustainability, Innovation, Integrity, and Collaboration guide every aspect of our operations.

SUSTAINABLE MINING PRACTICES

BHG Energy & Minerals operates under value-based principles of excellence, personal accountability, integrity and social and environmental responsibility.
