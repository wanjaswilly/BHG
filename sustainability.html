Our Sustainability Framework

At BHG Energy & Minerals, sustainability is integrated into every aspect of our operations. We go beyond compliance to implement innovative solutions that minimize environmental impact while maximizing social and economic benefits.

Environmental Stewardship

Implementing cutting-edge technologies to reduce our ecological footprint and promote biodiversity conservation.

Community Partnership

Working hand-in-hand with local communities to create shared value and sustainable development opportunities.

Clean Energy Transition

Providing critical minerals for renewable energy technologies while reducing our own carbon emissions.

Environmental Initiatives

Water Conservation

Implementing closed-loop water systems that recycle 85% of process water across our operations, significantly reducing freshwater consumption.

Land Rehabilitation

Progressive rehabilitation programs that restore mined areas to stable, productive ecosystems, often exceeding regulatory requirements.

Emissions Reduction

Transitioning to hybrid and electric mining vehicles, with a goal of 40% reduction in greenhouse gas emissions by 2027.

Community Impact

Education Programs

Funding scholarships and building schools to serve communities near our operations, with 1,200 students supported annually.
